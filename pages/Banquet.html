How to Go?

By MRT (Recommeded): The Taipei Metro Route Map is available here.

  1. From the Conference Venue to the MRT Station:
    Walk to Exit 5 of Chiang Kai-Shek Memorial Hall Station.
    (See walking route on Google Maps)
  2. Boarding the MRT:
    At Chiang Kai-Shek Memorial Hall Station, take the Green Line train heading toward "Songshan" direction.
    (cost approximately 12 minutes and TWD $20)
  3. Arrival at Destination Station:
    Get off at Nanjing Fuxing Station.
  4. Exit & Walking Directions to Restaurant:
    Exit the station via Exit 6. After reaching ground level, walk straight for about 100 meters (1 minute).
    You will see ChinaPa Restaurant.
    (See walking route on Google Maps)
